Platform

Built around daily clinical operations.

OpenEMR supports the core work described throughout the repository: electronic records, practice management, scheduling, billing, patient engagement, and interoperability.

01

Patient records

Manage encounters, demographics, notes, medications, allergies, documents, and longitudinal history from a single chart.

02

Scheduling

Coordinate providers, facilities, appointments, reminders, and calendar workflows for busy practices.

03

Billing

Support coding, claims, payments, reports, and practice management tasks in the same application.

04

Interoperability

Use APIs, FHIR support, CCD documents, and export tooling to keep health information moving responsibly.
